Help

This app enables Dungeon Masters to control ambient sounds, music and sound effects during a game session.

Ambient Sounds: Select an ambient category (e.g. Forest, City) to load tracks. Click any track to play it.

Music: Click a music type (Relax, Sad, Battle, Mystical) to load tracks. Then click any track to play it.

SFX: Each row has a play button and a "Set" button. Click "Set" to choose an SFX from the list. After choosing, the play button shows the SFX name; click it to play the sound.

Volume: Adjust the ambient, music and SFX volumes separately using the sliders.
