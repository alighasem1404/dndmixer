Help

This app enables Dungeon Masters to control music and sound effects during a game session.

Music: Click a music type (Relax, Sad, Battle, Mystical) to load the tracks. Then click any track to play it.

SFX: Each row has a play button and a "Set" button. Click "Set" to choose an SFX from the list. After choosing, the play button shows the SFX name; click it to play the sound.

Volume: Adjust the music and SFX volume separately using the sliders at the bottom.
